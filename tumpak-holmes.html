One day, when Holmes and I were discussing how we might get ourselves a job, the housemaid came in:

“Mr. Fullam wants to see you,” she said.

“Let him in,” responded Holmes.

In walked Randy Fullam, a security guard at the museum. He looked worried and excited.

“The Ruby — the Aztec Ruby was — was stolen from the museum!!!” he panted.
