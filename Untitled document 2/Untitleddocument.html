Shepherd Fullam’s

And the Potion of Azkaban

SCENE 1

We see HARRY at the hall of Hogwarts, he has just finished the Welcoming Feast and is chatting with his friends.

CUT to RON

RON: Are you sure you want to continue Potions?

CUT to HARRY

HARRY: Of course I am! It can always be useful. (Even if Snape teaches it)

SCENE 2

TIME CUT

HARRY is at his first Potions lesson. His potion is going fairly well when suddenly–

VOICE OF VOLDEMORT(in Parseltongue): Harry Potter…

ZOOM in on HARRY

His scar is searing!

FADE TO

SCENE 3

TIME CUT

HARRY, RON, and HERMIONE are walking to lunch after Double Potions with the Slytherins. They are reminiscing when HARRY remembers his scar:

HARRY: Wait — my scar really hurt during that lesson and I heard Volde–

RON: DON’T SAY THE NAME!

HARRY(defiant): Mort.

HARRY(cont’d): I heard his voice in my head during that lesson! And he was… speaking Parseltongue.
